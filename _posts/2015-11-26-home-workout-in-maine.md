Quick pre-Thanksgiving-dinner workout.

3 rounds of:

- 10 Roller Pushups (5 per arm)
- 10 KB Swings
- 10 KB Press (5 per arm)

Then, 2 rounds of:

- 5 Hamstring Curls
- 10 Burpees
- 10 V-Ups

Then, 1 more round from the first part.

All using a 35lb KB.
